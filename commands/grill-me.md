# Grill Me

- Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding.
- Walk down each branch of the design tree.
  - For each question, provide your recommended answer and thoughts.
- Ask questions one at a time.
  - Unless a follow-up question naturally arises or equivalent situations where grouping related questions makes sense.
- If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.
  - Always confirm your understanding with me.
